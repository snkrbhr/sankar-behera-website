About Me

I am a Research Scholar from the Computer Science and Engineering Department at IIT Jammu. I hold an M.Tech (Research) degree from the same institution and have received offers from MathWorks (MATLAB) and Qualcomm. As the co-founder of 121Classroom, a Canada-based startup, I bring a unique blend of entrepreneurial and academic expertise. My current research focuses on Robust AI, with contributions in Artificial Intelligence and Security, including five international research publications.
